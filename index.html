FAQ

Common buyer questions. For exact runtime calculations, share your device list on WhatsApp.

How long will it run my fridge, TV, and lights?

Runtime depends on your fridge efficiency, TV wattage, number of bulbs and hours used. As a planning rule, a studio/1-bed “essentials” setup often uses ~0.8–1.4 kWh/day.

How fast does solar charging work?

Solar charging depends on panel wattage, sun quality, shading, and the unit’s solar input limit. Many users choose 400–600W of panels for daily top-ups in good sun.

Do you offer instalments?

Yes—instalments are available for approved buyers. A deposit is required, and ownership transfers after full payment.

Do you deliver in Ghana and DR Congo?

Yes. We coordinate delivery and can connect you with installer partners where available.
